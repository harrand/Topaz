tz::Vec3 tz::phys::ColliderAABB::get_offset_midpoint ( ) const

Retrieve the midpoint offset, meaning the midpoint between the offset minimum and the offset maximum.

If the box is a cube or square, this will be {0, 0, 0}, unaffected by the transform position.
